Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Your privacy matters. PingMeet is designed with privacy-first principles. All your data stays in your browser - we don't operate any servers that collect or store your information.

Data We Access

Data Type Purpose Storage
Calendar Events Display meetings & send reminders Local Only
OAuth Tokens Authenticate with Google/Microsoft Local Only
Email Address Display connected account Local Only
Meeting Duration Track time in meetings Local Only
AI API Keys Generate meeting insights Local Only Optional

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's Storage API. This includes:

  • Your calendar connection settings
  • OAuth tokens for calendar access
  • Reminder preferences
  • Meeting duration statistics
  • AI provider configuration (if enabled)

We do not have servers that store your data. When you uninstall the extension, all data is automatically deleted.

Third-Party Services

PingMeet connects to the following services only when you explicitly connect your calendars:

  • Google Calendar API - To fetch your Google Calendar events
  • Microsoft Graph API - To fetch your Outlook Calendar events
  • AI Providers (Optional) - OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google AI for meeting insights. Only used if you configure an API key.

What We DON'T Do

  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't share your data with third parties (except AI providers if you opt-in)
  • We don't track your browsing activity
  • We don't show advertisements
  • We don't collect analytics or telemetry

AI Insights (Optional Feature)

If you choose to enable AI-powered meeting insights:

  • Your meeting titles and times may be sent to your chosen AI provider
  • You provide your own API key - we don't have access to it
  • This feature is completely optional and disabled by default
  • You can remove your API key at any time to disable this feature

Permissions Explained

Permission Why We Need It
identity OAuth authentication with Google/Microsoft
notifications Show meeting reminder notifications
alarms Schedule reminders at the right time
storage Save your settings and calendar data locally
tabs Open meeting links when you click "Join"
offscreen Play reminder sound in background
downloads Export weekly meeting reports

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the extension
  • Delete: Disconnect calendars or uninstall to remove all data
  • Portability: Export your meeting reports anytime

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
